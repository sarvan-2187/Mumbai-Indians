Mumbai Indians - IPL Trophy Wins

1. IPL 2013 – First Blood

Captain: Rohit Sharma (took over midway, never looked back!)

Final: MI vs CSK

Result: MI won by 23 runs

Venue: Eden Gardens, Kolkata

This win was special – the beginning of a dynasty and the end of the "no trophy" jokes.

2. IPL 2015 – Return of the Kings

Captain: Rohit Sharma

Final: MI vs CSK (Again!)

Result: MI won by 41 runs

Venue: Eden Gardens (again, lucky charm?)

A true comeback story – after a rough start to the season, MI stormed back to the top.

3. IPL 2017 – Nail-Biter

Captain: Rohit Sharma

Final: MI vs Rising Pune Supergiant

Result: MI won by 1 run

Venue: Rajiv Gandhi Intl. Stadium, Hyderabad

Probably the most thrilling IPL final ever – that 1-run win is the stuff of legend!

4. IPL 2019 – Deja Vu Drama

Captain: Rohit Sharma

Final: MI vs CSK (these two again!)

Result: MI won by 1 run (yes, again!)

Venue: Hyderabad

Two things became clear: MI loved Hyderabad, and they really loved winning finals by 1 run.

5. IPL 2020 – Pandemic Champions

Captain: Rohit Sharma

Final: MI vs Delhi Capitals

Result: MI won by 5 wickets

Venue: Dubai (due to COVID-19)

Dominated the whole season – cool, composed, clinical. Easily one of the most balanced MI squads.

Legacy of MI

Captain Fantastic: Rohit Sharma – the man with the Midas touch

Coach: Mahela Jayawardene (strategic genius)

Star Players over the years: Lasith Malinga, Jasprit Bumrah, Kieron Pollard, Hardik Pandya, Suryakumar Yadav, and the list goes on...

Known for building strong Indian cores and trusting their youngsters.

Fun Fact

MI’s title wins came in odd-numbered years till 2020. (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020).

After 2020, fans hoped for another title, but MI’s form has fluctuated. Still, with a solid fan base and strong leadership, they’re always title contenders.
